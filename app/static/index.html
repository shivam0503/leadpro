How It Works

From first message to
enrolled student

1
Lead lands on your website
The LeadPro widget auto-loads on your website. Visitor clicks WhatsApp or fills the enquiry form — instantly captured in your CRM.
2
AI counsellor takes over WhatsApp
Twilio webhook fires. The AI reads the message, checks your knowledge base, and replies in seconds — with the right course, fee, and CTA.
3
Lead is scored & tracked
Every message exchange updates the lead score. Hot leads alert your team. Full conversation history is viewable in the dashboard.
4
Counsellor closes the deal
Your team replies manually when needed, assigns leads, and syncs everything to LeadSquared — all from one dashboard.

Multi-tenant SaaS

One platform,
unlimited companies

Each company is a fully isolated tenant — separate database, custom AI, and its own WhatsApp setup.

���️

Isolated Databases

Every company gets its own SQLite DB at dbs/{slug}.db. Zero data leakage between tenants. Master DB tracks companies and super admins only.

���

Custom AI Personas

Each company trains the AI with their own website content and system prompt. Switch between Hinglish, English, and Arabic with one API call.

���

Per-company WhatsApp

Each tenant has their own Twilio Account SID, auth token, and WhatsApp number. Inbound webhooks are routed by company slug automatically.

���

Super Admin Control

Create companies, set plans, manage user limits, and monitor all tenants from a single super admin dashboard. Full RBAC with JWT tokens.
